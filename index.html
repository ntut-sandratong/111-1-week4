THE VEGAN LIFE

Koke Lab is a Hong Kong online-based vegan pastry shop founded in 2018.
Growing up in an environment closed to the nature, and after graduated from design school, Koke delved into the world of art and vegan pastry with the love for nature.
She founded the brand to tell stories, to please one's appetite and soul in ways that would affect the environment in the least extent.
We aspire to create cakes as diminutive and enticing as lichen in the forests.
